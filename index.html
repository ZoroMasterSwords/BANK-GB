Are you a victim of gender based violence?

GBV has several definitions, however it can be broadly defined as "violence that arises as a result of the normative role expectations associated with each gender, as well as uneven power relationships between genders, within the framework of a certain society." The expectations connected with different genders change throughout time and from society to civilization. Many societies have patriarchal power systems, in which male leadership is considered as the norm and men wield the bulk of authority. Patriarchy is a social and political system that views men as superior to women, in which women are unable to protect their bodies, achieve their basic needs, or fully participate in society, and men are free to abuse women.
